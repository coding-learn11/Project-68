Campus Life

Admissions/Parents Area

Best Place for Parents to Relax in School

The Admissions/Parents Area is large area open to all parents. It is cold there and has plenty of seating area. You can eat snacks and drink water too. Many awards are displayed there and you can also look through books, magazines, and school yearbooks. If you are planning to put your child into S.P.A., the admissions office is also there.

School Library

best place to do Class Meetings/Projects/Homework.

The School Library is a common area where students go to check out books, do homework, complete projects, and have meetings together. New books come into the school every week and there is also a suggestion box for books. The library is complete with two meeting rooms and a open meeting area for students and teachers to have meetings in.

School Amphitheatre

Best Place Make Announcements

The School Amphitheatre is where students practice radio talks. There are announcements made there and secondary students have allotted time to do voice plays and things like that. Even music is sometimes played there. Since the amphitheater is located in the middle of the school, it makes it easier to listen and pay attention to the announcements.

school achievements

World Scholars Cup
Winning Multiple Awards in the World Scholars Cup

Our school has won multiple awards in the final round of the World Scholars Cup. The World's Scholar Cup is an international team academic program with more than 15,000 students participating from over 65 countries every year. You will be required to participate in debates, write detailed masterpieces, and work out test papers all together as a team or individually.

Singapore & Asian Schools Math Olympiad
Winning Multiple Awards in Different Grade Levels

Our school's students have won places in SASMO or Singapore & Asian Schools Math Olympiad. Ranging from grades 3 to 11, our students have won bronze, silver, and gold medals, making us and their parents truly proud of them. SASMO is a math olympiad contest that stretches not only the brightest students but is aimed at instilling confidence for all students.

Indonesian Online Music Festival
Winning Multiple Awards in different sections of the Indonesian Online Music Festival

Our young students have won multiple awards for various sections in the Indonesia Online Music Festival. The sections that they have won in are violin, piano, and vocal. Hosted by PK entertainment, one of the biggest concert promoters in Indonesia, IOMF is one of the biggest music festivals held in Indonesia complete with fan meets, talks with people within the industry, and more.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsibilities to consider, canteens must be better organised and more efficient than they have been in the past. Online menus with photographs of the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Account on the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any dietary requirements they may have. Ordering online by ticking a box and paying for meals online can eliminate all the fuss of money handling, making it safer and less complicated for staff and students alike. Here again, diversity is the key.

  • New Classrooms

    The school has also recently updated the classrooms to make the students' learning environment more effective and productive. New tables and chairs have been placed, the new whiteboards are as clean as they could be and the replaced teachers desks provides more space for the teachers to keep their stuff. As a result, the classrooms now look much cleaner and modern.
